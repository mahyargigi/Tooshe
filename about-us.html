About us

Tooshe is a collaborative community that connects travelers with people with shipping needs ( like sending or buying Products all around the world.)

It is a Persian startup founded by Students Of Sharif University OF Technology.

A fast and easy process that offers an flexible alternative to the typical shipping and posting services by connecting people with shipping needs with travelers who are willing to carry an extra package in a exchange for a reward. Who carry earn, who send save.

Want to send something to your cousin who lives across town or to your friend who lives in another country without the usual expensive fees? Do you have free space on your luggage and want to make some extra cash?

We realized we could match both, travelers and senders, to create a way of helping each other with advantages for all involved.

Tooshe would not interfere with the proposed and accepted rewards among senders and travelers, however we will take a small fee if they opt to use our multiple security options, insurance cover, and payment protection process.

Tooshe is a network made by people for people, but at the end the users are the ones who choose who they want to share the experience, we'll just make it happen.
